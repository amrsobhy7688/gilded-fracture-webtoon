## Main Protagonists

### Vesper Aurelius

**Role:** Divine-social lens into the Empire’s elegant machinery.  
**Player:** Amr  
**Primary Visual Motifs:** white, cream, gold, celestial accessories, divine light, perception threads.  
**Story Function:** Noble office, sanctity compliance, magical perception, social power.

### Leo Gray

**Role:** Street-level investigative blade.  
**Player:** Sarah  
**Primary Visual Motifs:** simple white shirt, case notes, hidden seams, forged documents, noir investigator posture.  
**Story Function:** Covert legwork, private investigation, document theft, crime scene analysis.
