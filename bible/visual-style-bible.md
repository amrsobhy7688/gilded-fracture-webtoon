# Visual Style Bible

**Style Direction:** Anime-inspired, cinematic, vibrant, luminous, elegant, mystery-forward.

---

## 1. Overall Style

Anime cinematic fantasy noir.

The art should combine:

- Clean expressive anime faces.
- Elegant costume silhouettes.
- Highly polished magical architecture.
- Vibrant but controlled color.
- Sharp contrast between sacred public spaces and hidden service infrastructure.

---

| Element | Palette | Use |

| Cyritium public face | White, gold, warm cream, pale crystal blue | Ministries, noble offices, festival infrastructure |
| Solaria / Vesper faith | Sun-gold, ivory, pearl, champagne | Vesper, Sun Scouring, divine magic |
| Leo / private investigation | Ink black, warm brown, muted white, smoke grey | Tea house, apartment, covert work |

---

## 3. Recurring Visual Devices

### The Ledger

- Ink becomes city streets.
- Columns become panel borders.
- Missing entries become black voids.
- Stamps become sound effects.

### Light as Bureaucracy

Light should not always feel holy. Sometimes it should feel like surveillance.

Use:

- Crystal halos around official documents.
- Sunlight forming grids.
- Golden seals that resemble eyes.
- Polished floors reflecting people like evidence.

### Thought Reading

- Surface thoughts appear as thin, pale text behind the face.
- Deep probing uses invasive light threads.
- Mutual mind-reading uses mirrored panels.
- Failed probing creates a hard glass-like fracture across the panel.

### The Number 46

- Forty-six ledger gaps.
- Forty-six crystal pedestals.
- Forty-six dots in page border designs.
- CL-A-46-D scratched in a tiny but unforgettable panel.
