## 1. Episode Purpose

Introduce Vesper Aurelius, her new office in Cyritium, House Aurelian’s bureaucratic world, Petre, and the first appearance of the forty-six crystal assignment from her side.

---

## 2. Required Canon Facts

- Vesper has a newly prepared office in Cyritium.
- The office is elegant, white, gold, crystalline, and almost like a refined magical clinic.
- Vesper sanctified the office with a religious ceremony.
- A plush toy sits openly on her desk, visually out of place.
- Petre enters with tea and an envelope.
- Petre is young, military-postured, and assigned to manage appointments/correspondence.
- The envelope includes a personal handwritten note from Lord Aurelian.
- The assignment is to observe and cosign the Festival Crystal Calibration Ceremony.
- The assignment concerns forty-six tier-three festival infrastructure crystals.
- Vesper reads Petre’s surface thoughts and notices his professional wound around Lord Aurelian’s personal letter.
