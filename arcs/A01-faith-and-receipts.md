# Arc A01: Faith and Receipts

**Season:** SE01  
**Source:** SRC-S01, Session 1 Notes  
**Status:** In Design  
**Core Mystery:** Forty-six crystals are missing from the Celestial Ledger.  
**Primary POVs:** Vesper Aurelius, Leo Gray

---

## 1. Arc Function

Arc A01 introduces the core machinery of the campaign:

- The Empire as beautiful bureaucracy.
- The missing forty-six crystals.
- Leo’s covert street-level investigation.
- Vesper’s noble/institutional entry point.
- The first murder: Eren Voss.
- The first major institutional cover-up.
- The bridge into Merchant Review.

This arc should feel like a golden door opening into a basement full of clean bloodstains.

## 5. Visual Identity

Arc A01 is built on contrast:

- White/gold official spaces.
- Grey/blue service infrastructure.
- Warm festival light.
- Cold procedural cleanup.
- Beautiful documents with terrifying implications.

Recurring motifs:

- Ledger columns.
- Forty-six gaps.
- Stamps and seals.
- Crystal hums.
- Hidden folded paper.
- Gloves and evidence bags.
- Cleaned surfaces.
- Gold light behaving like surveillance.

---

## 6. Ending State

By the end of A01:

- Vesper suspects House Aurelian’s role is not neutral.
- Leo has evidence that Eren Voss was covering or tracking missing crystals.
- The reader knows the official story is false.
- The next objective is to get into Merchant Review.
