The prologue is treated as **Arc S00**.

It introduces the whole story engine:

- The Cyriath Empire’s obsession with light, order, sanctity, records, and proof.
- Cyritium as the capital of divine bureaucracy.
- Ascension Week as the most orderly and beautiful time of the year.
- The Celestial Ledger as sacred infrastructure.
- The first fracture: **forty-six crystals are missing**.

This arc should feel like a radiant machine quietly producing an impossible error.

# S00-E01: The Ledger and the Missing Forty-Six

## Metadata

**Status:** Panel Description Draft  
**Main POV:** Omniscient  
**Estimated Panels:** 60  
**Scroll Sections:** 8  
**Format:** Vertical-scroll webtoon, phone-first

---

## Episode Purpose

This episode is the **cold open** of the webtoon.

It should not feel like a history lesson. It should feel like the reader is being invited into a radiant machine that is already making a tiny, fatal accounting error.

The episode establishes:

- The Cyriath Empire’s sacred-bureaucratic identity.
- The Celestial Ledger as proof of belonging.
- Cyritium as a city of verification, sanctity, and records.
- The beauty of Ascension Week.
- The first crack in the system: **forty-six crystals are missing**.

---

## Phone-First Vertical Format Rules

This episode is designed for mobile reading, not page spreads.

### Core Layout Rules

- Use a **single vertical reading column**.
- Avoid wide comic-page compositions that require zooming.
- Each panel should read clearly on a phone without pinching.
- Text should be large, sparse, and placed in clean negative space.
- Long lore becomes visual systems: ledgers, seals, broadcasts, city rhythm.
- Use **gutters as timing**.
- Every scroll section should have one dominant visual image.

### Suggested Canvas Logic

- Work in vertical strips by scroll section.
- Each scroll section can be drawn/exported separately.
- Use repeated gold-white-black visual rhythm:
  - Gold = order / sanctity / propaganda.
  - White = civic purity / official space.
  - Black = absence / missing records / hidden rot.

### Text Density Rule

For most panels:

- 0 to 1 caption.
- Maximum 2 short caption lines.
- Important phrases get isolated panels.
- Avoid large paragraphs.

## S00-E01-S01: Name Written in Light

**Section Goal:** Open with sacred identity. The reader should understand that citizenship is not emotional or patriotic first. It is recorded, measured, and sanctified.

### S00-E01-S01-P001

**Panel Type:** Tall black opening panel.  
**Visual:** Full black vertical panel. A single hair-thin gold line appears from top to bottom, like sunlight used as ink. It should feel quiet and ceremonial, not explosive.  
**Text:** `In the year 643 post-Unmaking...`  
**Phone Note:** Let the black panel breathe. Use a long gutter before and after.

### S00-E01-S01-P002

**Panel Type:** Close abstract panel.  
**Visual:** The gold line curves into divine calligraphy. It is almost a name, but not readable yet. The letters feel alive, suspended in darkness.  
**Text:** `Your name is not written in ink.`

### S00-E01-S01-P003

**Panel Type:** Extreme close-up.  
**Visual:** A human eye, warm brown or dark grey, reflecting the glowing name. The reflected letters ripple across the iris like a registry spell.  
**Text:** `Your name is written in light.`

### S00-E01-S01-P004

**Panel Type:** Medium vertical reveal.  
**Visual:** Pull back to show a citizen standing at a luminous registry desk. A clerk in pale official robes checks a floating ledger projection. The citizen stands still, almost prayerfully.  
**Text:** `To belong to the Cyriath Empire is to be recorded.`

### S00-E01-S01-P005

**Panel Type:** Domestic detail panel.  
**Visual:** Morning tea on a modest but clean table. Beside it, a small aether meter hums on the wall. Tiny gold figures float above the meter.  
**Text:** `Your household.`

### S00-E01-S01-P006

**Panel Type:** Bureaucratic detail panel.  
**Visual:** A ministry hand runs a stylus down a glowing line of household aether usage. The line is precise, beautiful, and invasive.

### S00-E01-S01-P007

**Panel Type:** Split vertical comparison.  
**Visual:** A noble crest and a common citizen seal appear in adjacent ledger columns. They are visually different but locked into the same grid.  
**Text:** `Noble or common, everyone has a place in the illuminated whole.`

**Panel Type:** Transforming transition panel.  
**Visual:** The ledger line extends downward and becomes a glowing city road. The reader scrolls from a page into the city itself.

### S00-E01-S07-P050

## S00-E01-S08: Forty-Six

**Section Goal:** Convert the small error into the core mystery. The final panels should feel like a trapdoor opening beneath the golden city.

### S00-E01-S08-P056

**Panel Type:** Compliance pressure panel.  
**Visual:** Towers of reports. Clerks moving fast. A stamp line waiting. The city has too much official work to notice one anomaly.  
**Text:** `Not when every house’s compliance report is due.`

### S00-E01-S08-P057

**Panel Type:** Broadcast dominance panel.  
**Visual:** Radiant Broadcast text glows above the city, visually overpowering the tiny missing ledger line beneath it.  
**Text:** `Not when the Radiant Broadcast is speaking.`

### S00-E01-S08-P058

**Panel Type:** Law and light panel.  
**Visual:** A legal tablet, a temple sun-disc, and a gold-lit street align into one rigid vertical composition. Beautiful, absolute, suffocating.  
**Text:** `Not when the law is absolute and the light is gold.`

### S00-E01-S08-P059

**Panel Type:** Full reveal panel.  
**Visual:** Return to the ledger. Forty-six missing entries are now visible as black gaps in a luminous page. Arrange the gaps so the reader can feel the count without needing to count each one.  
**Text:** `Except...`  
**Phone Note:** Long pause before this panel. Let the reader scroll into the black gaps.

### S00-E01-S08-P060

**Panel Type:** Final cliffhanger panel.  
**Visual:** A closed ledger floats in darkness. Around it, forty-six tiny gold sparks extinguish one by one. The last spark leaves a black dot reflected in the ledger’s gold seal.  
**Final Text:** `Forty-six crystals are missing from the ledger.`  
**End Beat:** No extra explanation after this. End hard.
