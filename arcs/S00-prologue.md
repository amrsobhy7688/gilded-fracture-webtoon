# Arc S00: Prologue

**Source:** Session notes prologue  
**Status:** Designed  
**Primary POV:** Omniscient  
**Core Arc Function:** Establish the series premise before the active Session 1 investigation begins.

---

## 1. Arc Function

The prologue is treated as **Arc S00**.

It is not inside Session 1 because it frames the whole story, but it still follows the same arc-file structure as every other campaign/session arc.

It introduces:

- The Cyriath Empire’s obsession with light, order, sanctity, records, and proof.
- Cyritium as the capital of divine bureaucracy.
- Ascension Week as the most orderly and beautiful time of the year.
- The Celestial Ledger as sacred infrastructure.
- The first fracture: **forty-six crystals are missing**.

This arc should feel like a radiant machine quietly producing an impossible error.

## 3. Episode Design Library

# S00-E01: The Ledger and the Missing Forty-Six

## Metadata

**Status:** Designed  
**Main POV:** Omniscient  
**Estimated Panels:** 60  
**Scroll Sections:** 8

---

## Episode Purpose

This episode is the **cold open** of the webtoon.

It should not feel like a history lesson. It should feel like the reader is being invited into a radiant machine that is already making a tiny, fatal accounting error.

The episode establishes:

- The Cyriath Empire’s sacred-bureaucratic identity.
- The Celestial Ledger as proof of belonging.
- Cyritium as a city of verification, sanctity, and records.
- The beauty of Ascension Week.
- The first crack in the system: **forty-six crystals are missing**.

---

## Reader Promise

By the end of S00-E01, the reader should understand:

> This world is beautiful, holy, efficient, and deeply dangerous because its violence hides inside procedure.

The emotional flavor:

- Awe first.
- Comfort second.
- Unease third.
- Dread last.
