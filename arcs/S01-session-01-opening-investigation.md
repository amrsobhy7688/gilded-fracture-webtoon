---

## 1. Arc Function

This session arc adapts the first active investigation after the series prologue.

It introduces:

- Leo’s private-investigator thread through Felda and Marshal Bren Sable.
- Vesper’s House Aurelian thread through her new office and calibration assignment.
- The first overlap: **forty-six**.
- The first crime scene: Third Junction.
- The first institutional cover-up.
- The first evidence chain leading toward Merchant Review.

# S01-E01: Leo at the Tea House

## Metadata

**Status:** Planned  
**Main POV:** Leo Gray  
**Source Scene:** Leo at the Tea House  
**Estimated Panels:** TBD

## Episode Purpose

Introduce Leo Gray and the first human crack in the imperial ledger mystery.

## Required Canon Facts

- Leo meets Felda in a quiet tea house.
- Felda accuses him of delay: “You said three days.”
- Leo answers: “Four.”
- Felda leaves cash and deliberately leaves behind a hidden note beneath the saucer.
- The note reads: “The clerk is asking about the forty-sixth line again. It has been handled. Do not involve anyone else.”
- Leo returns to his apartment and cannot find/remember Felda’s exact job details.
- A Gilded Guard runner knocks on the window, not the door.
- Leo receives a direct handwritten note from Marshal Bren Sable.
- Bren asks Leo to investigate Eren Voss’s death at Third Junction covertly.

# S01-E02: Vesper’s First Day in Cyritium

## Metadata

**Status:** Planned  
**Main POV:** Vesper Aurelius  
**Source Scene:** Vesper’s First Day in Cyritium  
**Estimated Panels:** TBD

## Episode Purpose

Introduce Vesper Aurelius, her new office in Cyritium, House Aurelian’s bureaucratic world, Petre, and the first appearance of the forty-six crystal assignment from her side.

## Required Canon Facts

- Vesper has a newly prepared office in Cyritium.
- The office is elegant, white, gold, crystalline, and almost like a refined magical clinic.
- Vesper sanctified the office with a religious ceremony.
- A plush toy sits openly on her desk, visually out of place.
- Petre enters with tea and an envelope.
- Petre is young, military-postured, and assigned to manage appointments/correspondence.
- The envelope includes a personal handwritten note from Lord Aurelian.
- The assignment is to observe and cosign the Festival Crystal Calibration Ceremony.
- The assignment concerns forty-six tier-three festival infrastructure crystals.
- Vesper reads Petre’s surface thoughts and notices his professional wound around Lord Aurelian’s personal letter.
